Privacy Policy for No YouTube Spoilers

No YouTube Spoilers does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information the extension handles

  • User-defined keywords are stored locally using the browser’s storage system.
  • No data is sent to external servers.
  • No analytics, tracking, or logging is performed.

Permissions

The extension requests the minimum permissions required to function:

  • activeTab – to apply filters to YouTube pages only when active.
  • scripting – to run filtering logic on YouTube.
  • storage – to save the user’s keyword lists.
  • Host permission for youtube.com – to modify YouTube content according to user settings.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party.

Contact

If you have questions, you may contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
